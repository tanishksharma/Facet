Icon button

A square button holding a single icon: the clear cross in a number field, a close button, a share arrow. The icon alone says nothing, so every icon button carries an aria-label for screen readers and a data-tip tooltip for everyone else. Combines with any button variant and size.

Variants

Sizes and states

Small stays 36px next to text but grows back to the full 44px touch target on touch screens. Tab to one for the focus ring.

Snippet

<button class="btn btn-icon" aria-label="Close" data-tip="Close this panel">
  <svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 20 20" fill="none"
       stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.5" stroke-linecap="round"
       aria-hidden="true">
    <path d="m5 5 10 10M15 5 5 15"/>
  </svg>
</button>

Icons are inline SVG with stroke="currentColor", so they take the button's text color in every theme, light and dark. The aria-hidden on the SVG keeps screen readers on the aria-label.
