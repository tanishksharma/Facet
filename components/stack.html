Stack

Vertical flow with themed gaps. A stack says one thing: these sit above and below each other, this far apart. The gaps come from the spacing tokens, so a theme change restyles the rhythm of the page, not just the components inside it.

Three gaps, live

.stack-tight

like a label and its field

.stack

The default flow

for content inside a section

.stack-loose

Whole sections of a page

gap: --space-7
