Button

Three variants, three sizes. One primary per screen — it carries the accent and the screen's main verb. Everything else is secondary, and quiet actions inside busy areas go ghost. A .btn works the same on <button> and on <a>.

The data-event attribute is the analytics hook: name the button's key action there and any analytics tool can listen for it.
