Container

The page-width wrapper. It centers content, caps its width, and keeps themed side padding, so one theme flip restyles the layout too. Use it directly on semantic tags: main, header, footer, section. Never nest a container in a container.

Every width, live

The dashed edges show each container's bounds inside this page.

.container-narrow · 40rem · prose, forms, calculators
.container · 64rem · most pages
.container-wide · 80rem · dashboards, galleries
.container-full · edge to edge, side padding kept

Snippet

<main class="container">
  Page content sits here, centered, capped at 64rem,
  with themed side padding.
</main>
