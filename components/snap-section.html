Full-viewport scroll-snap areas: the calculator page skeleton. The page scrolls one full screen at a time — intro, then inputs, then results — and each section centers its content at a readable width. The .snap wrapper exists to enable scrolling, which is the one reason the wrapper law allows a container in a container.
