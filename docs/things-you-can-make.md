# Things you can make

The business artifacts the library produces, on screen and on paper at exact size. Each rides the print system and the document templates.

## Cards

Business card, conference badge, ID card, name tag.

## Pitch deck

Exact 1920x1080, presents with keys, prints to PDF. Many slide layouts:

- Title. The opening slide.
- Agenda. What is coming.
- Big statement. One line, full slide.
- Split. Text beside image.
- Full-bleed image. With one caption line.
- Data. A chart with its takeaway.
- Table. The numbers slide.
- Quote. One voice, attributed.
- Team. Faces and roles.
- Timeline. The roadmap.
- Pricing. The plans.
- Comparison. Features against alternatives.
- Market. The sizing slide.
- Closing. The ask and the contact.

## Letters

Letterhead, formal letter, cover letter, offer letter, memo, press release, compliment slip, envelope.

## Money documents

Invoice, quote, estimate, purchase order, receipt, statement of account, credit note, packing slip, price list, rate card, expense report, timesheet.

## Business documents

Proposal, contract, NDA, statement of work, report, business plan, whitepaper, case study, one-pager, spec sheet, media kit, agenda, minutes, SOP, handbook, intake form, waiver.

## Food and retail

Dine-in menu, takeout menu, specials card, shelf price sign, product label, shipping label, thermal receipt, catalogue.

## Promotion

Flyer, poster, brochure, signage, banner artwork.

## Certificates

Training certificate, award certificate, with border, seal and signature line.

## Structured sheets

Org chart, roadmap one-pager, shift rota, attendance sheet, content calendar.

## The primitives behind all of it

- Page-size registry. Every needed paper and card size.
- N-up print sheets. Many small pieces per sheet, aligned.
- Fold lines. Panels placed and rotated for folding.
- Form lines. Write-in rules, pen checkboxes, signature lines.
- Running heads. Page numbers and headers on long documents.
- Serial numbers. Numbered runs for tickets and receipts.
- QR generator. The bridge from paper to its web twin.
- Connectors. The lines in org charts and trees.
- Bleed and large format. Print-shop trim and poster sizes.
