Tune the semantic tokens and watch a few real layouts restyle, then copy the config into your project's :root. Below, Skin Lab shows every theme in light and dark, side by side.

Pick your tokens

Build on top of the active theme, one token category at a time — colors, spacing, type and shape — previewed live on a canvas of real layouts beside the controls. Colors derive their hover, pressed and on-shades automatically; the numeric tokens use a detailed slider (drag coarse, then fine-tune with the −/+ or by typing). The build rides this page's URL, so a shared link opens on your exact tokens.
